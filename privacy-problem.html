Privacy: The greatest technical Problem

Introduction

As everyone knows, privacy has been nowhere now. Most average people don't even know what a VPN is, does not browse with protection, searches without knowing that services use their information to target them with ads and trackers to monitor everything they do. Here is why Privacy is the greatest technical problem and one of the Global problems in the digital age.

Reasons why privacy is a global problem


Being too open

Today, we are innocently giving away a lot of information in searching online, and browsing through social networks. I admit myself, giving away personal information before I got knowledge on Information Technology. I shared a lot in Facebook posts, entered about a million+ queries in google search since 2012 to 2018. There is a lot of alternatives to these services, but not enough people are aware of these, and maybe just stick to data collecting services, and just trust them with their information despite past breaches, other services experienced.


Refusal of Privacy

A lot of people do not want to use alternatives to data collecting software. DuckDuckGo, Startpage.com, Gibiru, Mojeek, and many more are good examples of private alternatives to Google search. But many still wants google because it is at the top of giving good results. Signal, Viber, Telegram, Wire are also good, but they choose Messenger, and Facebook for social network just because they are popular. So, they stick to popular platforms for technology and refuse to use some of the alternatives because they can’t beat the popular tech.


Can’t just make them switch

Of course, you can’t force someone to join the privacy side, they will just ignore it. But they will soon understand why you are asking them to go to privacy. I myself, try to convince my friends and conducted research to prove them that they need to protect their privacy. So after educating them though our free time during online classes, they eventually gave their answers on if they trust Google and Facebook. And yes, they said NO.


Previous Scandals

According to news websites and other tech sites, Facebook had been caught collecting personal information and on behalf of the trump campaign in 2016. Their Cambridge Analytica scandal had made a change on all Americans’ view of privacy. Also They, and Google was recently caught in a monopoly of exchanging information and too target ads to target users. These scandals is why I have made PersonGuyknowsnotech websites, entirely of HTML and CSS, and currently improving. My websites really focus more on privacy and technology sometimes. I am a privacy Advocate, and my articles could bring life to people to change their perspective on tech, and change to the private side.


Snowden Revelations

This is probably the most reason why many are using Duckduckgo, Signal, TOR, Protonmail, and many more. Because of this, we have not let tech take over us and let the NSA track and spy on us whenever they want and however they want. The internet is not meant for collecting data and making money out of people’s lives. The internet is meant to share information and not to collect them.


Conclusion

Everything that we do and every information that we give, it is monitored. Privacy has become a global problem, and not enough are able to fix it. That is why, Be a privacy advocate, support the protection of everyone’s privacy, do not let anyone manipulate your data, control it. So many people are looking for privacy advocate, some may think they are crazy, but hear me out, these ones have contributed to protection of privacy as how Snowden became heroic. So, in conclusion in a nutshell, Privacy is a problem and we are the solution.
